SpaceWork 2024 expeditions. will take place in our state of the art Zeus 11 spacecraft equipped with comfortable living facilities and our signature viewing area. The craft will take off from our launch pad in Cape Canaveral, Florida on June 28th, 2024, and land 36 hours later at approximately noon on June 29th, 2024. Before take-off and our space viewing experience, all travelers must complete a training camp on space safety and emergency protocols from March 11th to March 18th, 2024. Alternate dates will be available upon request for those who are not able to attend.
