SpaceWork 2024 expeditions. will take place in our state of the art Zeus 11 spacecraft equipped with comfortable living facilities and our signature viewing area. The craft will take off from our launch pad in Cape Canaveral, Florida on June 28th, 2024, and land 36 hours later at approximately noon on June 29th, 2024. Before take-off and our space viewing experience, all travelers must complete a training camp on space safety and emergency protocols from March 11th to March 18th, 2024. Alternate dates will be available upon request for those who are not able to attend.
The seven day training program will ensure that all candidates are physically able to endure the strain of space travel. An at-home test will be sent to all potential candidates before the sign up process concludes. In order to ensure safety for all candidates, applicants should fill out this questionnaire honestly and completely. Good physical health is required for candidate safety and emergency protocols. Additional training will be provided for launch and landing protocols during the training camp. A team of professionals will be on standby throughout launch, atmosphere, and landing in order to ensure safety and efficiency.
