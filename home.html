Join us for a majestic trip through the Galaxy—SAFER THAN EVER.
Have you already seen what there is to see on Earth? Do you want to see sights you could previously only dream of? With SpaceWork, your dreams of exploring space and the galaxy can be made a reality.
SpaceWork enables civilians to take a 36-hour trip through our solar system, making viewing stops above the atmospheres of Jupiter and Venus and a short stop on the moon. Our craft Zeus 11 hosts a variety of passenger cabins including one, two, and four person options, as well as luxurious common spaces and a fully-equipped kitchen and restaurant. Travellers will enjoy 360 degree views from our state-of-the-art observatory, where professional cameras and telescopes are available for use. Before returning to Earth, guests will be trained as astronauts and take a short moonwalk on the surface of Earth’s moon.
